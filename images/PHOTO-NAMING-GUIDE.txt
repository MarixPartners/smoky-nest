THE SMOKY NEST — PHOTO NAMING GUIDE
====================================

HOW TO USE:
1. Put ALL your location photos in THIS folder (the "images" folder).
2. Name each photo using the filename listed below for that place.
3. Use .jpg or .png — either is fine. Just keep the name (left side) exactly.
4. Don't worry about photo size — Claude will optimize them for fast loading.
5. When they're all in, tell Claude "photos are in" and they'll be added to the site.

You don't have to provide every one. Any you skip will keep its current placeholder
(or you can ask Claude to remove the photo from that card).
